About Me

Experience that
complements, not replaces.

My name is Arnold Ruijter. With over 20 years in complex enterprise environments, my goal is to add experience, perspective, and leadership where and when it adds value.

I aim to leverage & embrace existing capability, rather than replacing it. In other words, strengthen what is already there and provide extra focus where it is needed.

"What you see is what you get" — I am open, direct, and transparent. I do not maintain half-truths, and everyone knows where to stand.

I started in IT without highly relevant studies — just a lot of curiosity. From dissecting Excel macros, to Visual Basic, to enterprise-wide architecture. In all those years, I developed my own vision on what I found are the key elements of business IT.

While much of my career has been at enterprise or strategic level, I am equally at home with domain architecture, cross-sector assignments, or engagements at any scale. The level of the role matters less than the quality of the outcome.

Core Philosophy

P

People

IT cannot succeed without the people who use and build it. I invest in understanding teams, coaching individuals, and building trust — because results follow people.

P

Process

Every organization does things a certain way, for a reason. Understanding those processes — and respecting them while enabling change — is at the heart of any transformation.

R

Result

Good architecture and strong teams only matter if they deliver. I keep the end goal in sight — translating effort into visible, meaningful progress that the organisation can see and build on.

What I Offer

Core Services

In my 30+ years of experience — including more than 20 years in large enterprises with complex processes and IT landscapes — I provide the following services:

🏛️

Enterprise & Domain Architecture

Analyse your organisation, understand business processes and the IT landscape, and design a coherent path forward — at enterprise level or scoped to a specific domain.

  • Analyse organisation structure with a focus on IT
  • Gather requirements from business and IT stakeholders
  • Record current architecture and design the future state
  • Build the roadmap and safeguard consistency during transformation
  • TOGAF-certified approach to architecture governance
  • Domain architecture for a specific IT area or capability
👥

IT Leadership

Build high-performing IT teams by understanding people, coaching strengths, and creating an environment of ownership and trust.

  • Understand team dynamics, expertise, and interests
  • Coach on strengths and personal ownership
  • Guide communication within the team and with stakeholders
  • Be a person of trust with an open-door approach
  • Enable self-direction — teams that tell you, not the other way around
🔗

Integration Architecture

Design robust and scalable integration landscapes for Supply Chain, Finance, Product Master, Logistics, and more.

  • Analyse organisation and IT landscape for integration gaps
  • Design integration architecture based on requirements
  • Apply EDI, EAI, API, and middleware patterns
  • Design or review mapping specifications and communication protocols
  • Maintain architecture consistency and documentation
🎯

IT Strategy

Translate business ambitions into a prioritised, realistic IT roadmap — aligned across teams and grounded in the organisation's goals.

  • Analyse opportunities for technology to grow the business
  • Determine critical path vs. nice-to-have
  • Guide people through change with clarity on the "Why"
  • Align business and IT vision into a single coherent strategy
  • Bring the tech and business roadmap together
  • Brainstorm with management and support business targets
💼

Advisory & Specialist Roles

Available for engagements beyond the traditional enterprise scope — from domain-level assignments to cross-sector advisory, at whatever scale best fits your situation.

  • Domain architecture within a specific system or IT area
  • Cross-sector engagements — from retail and fashion to public sector and beyond
  • Vendor selection, tendering support, and solution assessment
  • Smaller organisations that benefit from senior expertise on demand
  • Management advisory without a full-time or long-term commitment
